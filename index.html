📅 When To Trade

Wednesday Updates
Game updates often cause volatility — watch for opportunities when prices spike unexpectedly.
Weekends
More players online means higher volume. Prices tend to be lower when supply increases.
Monday Margins
Lower volume can mean wider spreads — good day to find items with better margins.
League Starts
Demand spikes for specific items 1-2 weeks before new seasonal leagues launch.
PvM Updates
Boss items spike after new content releases — watch the announcement schedule.

RuneLite Plugin

Flip directly from your game client. The Grand Flip Out plugin integrates into RuneLite's sidebar with live prices, realistic profit estimates, dump alerts, and session tracking.

Download Plugin (ZIP)
1
Download and extract the ZIP to any folder on your PC
2
Open RuneLite, click the wrench icon (Configuration) at top right
3
Enable "Developer Tools" under RuneLite settings
4
Click "Load External Plugin" and point to the extracted folder
5
The "Grand Flip Out" panel appears in your sidebar. You're ready!
