COMMANDS IMPLEMENTED:
=====================

1. /price <item_name>
   - Look up item price and margins
   - Fuzzy search by name
   - Shows current/buy/sell prices
   - Shows JTI score and margins
   - Displays item icon

2. /top [sort] [limit]
   - Top items by JTI (default)
   - Top items by margin
   - Top items by volume
   - Configurable limit (1-20)

3. /dumps
   - Items dropping >5% in 5 minutes
   - Real-time crash detection
   - Shows percentage and prices

4. /pumps
   - Items with unusual buy pressure
   - High buy volume detection
   - Sorted by trading activity

5. /recipe
   - Profitable recipes
   - Herb cleaning, gem cutting
   - Shows profit per item

6. /jti <item_name>
   - Detailed JTI breakdown
   - Recent price history
   - Profitability metrics

7. /market
   - Overall market statistics
   - Total volume
   - Average margins
   - Active items count

8. /compare <item1> <item2>
   - Side-by-side comparison
   - All key metrics
   - Easy differentiation

9. /watchlist [add|remove|show]
   - Per-user watchlist
   - JSON persistence
   - Add/remove items
   - Show watched prices
