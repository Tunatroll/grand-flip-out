Features

Live market data and a ranked list of flip opportunities, plus your own trade history and profit—all in RuneLite, with your data staying on your PC.

Live market & opportunities

Real-time GE prices, scored and ranked so you can see what's worth flipping. Pick a strategy: low risk (stable, high volume), high volume (fast flips), or high margin (bigger profit per trade). We also flag when volume or margins shift so you're not caught off guard.

What's in the Live Market tab

  • Buy price, sell price, and margin for every item
  • Ranked opportunities with confidence score and a plain-English reason (e.g. "High spread stability and volume")
  • Strategy label showing which ranking you're using
  • API status line so you always know if data is flowing

Your trade history & profit

Every buy and sell is recorded on your machine. Session profit, all-time profit, open positions, and a full flip log. See which items are making you money. None of that data is sent to us—it stays on your PC unless you export it.

What's in the Trade History tab

  • Session summary: profit, buys, sells, items traded
  • Open positions: what you're holding, average cost
  • Profit by item: ROI and margin per item
  • Recent trades: chronological buy/sell list
  • Flip log: every completed flip with timestamp

Export, import, and portability

Your data is yours. Copy it, save it, move it.

  • Copy/paste JSON for trades and flip logs (clipboard)
  • Export to CSV for spreadsheets
  • Save to file or load from file (JSON)
  • Clear trades or logs any time

Hotkeys & alerts

Set hotkeys to cycle tabs, start a new session, or refresh the panel. Turn on desktop notifications when a high-confidence opportunity appears—set your own margin and confidence thresholds so you only hear about the good ones.

Strategy presets

Choose how opportunities are ranked:

  • Default — balanced ranking (volume + margin + stability)
  • Low risk — high volume, stable spreads
  • High volume — fastest-moving items
  • High margin — biggest GP per flip

Plays by the rules

We only show you data and record what you do at the GE. No automation, no funny business. Your API key is stored as a secret. Built for the official RuneLite client and Jagex policy.
